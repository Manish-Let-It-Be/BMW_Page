BMW : Luxury Car Brand

BMW, or Bayerische Motoren Werke, is a prestigious German automobile manufacturer
known for its luxury vehicles, high-performance sports cars, and cutting-edge technology. 
Founded in 1916,BMW has built a reputation for producing cars that blend dynamic driving 
experience with sophisticated design. The brand is recognized worldwide for its precision 
engineering, powerful engines, and innovative features that cater to both performance 
enthusiasts and those seeking comfort and luxury. With iconic models such as the 3 Series, 
5 Series, and X5, BMW continues to push the boundaries of automotive excellence, blending 
tradition with forward-thinking sustainability through its electrified vehicles like the i3 and i8.
